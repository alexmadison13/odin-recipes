Recipe servings and timeline:

  • Prep time: 30 min
  • Cook time: 2 hrs 30 min
  • Additional time; 15 min
  • Total time: 3 hrs 15 min
  • Servings: 12

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion & garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions below.
  5. Cover with foli and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to layer lasagna:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
